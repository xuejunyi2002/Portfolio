What I Bring

Some of my strengths as a creative producer

Structure that keeps teams moving.

I turn ambitious ideas into clear production plans, connecting scope, milestones, priorities, and team capacity so creative teams can stay focused and move forward with confidence.

  • Sprint planning, retrospectives, and milestone tracking
  • Scope definition and proactive prioritization
  • Risk, dependency, and workload visibility
  • Clear documentation across the production lifecycle
  • Review, playtest, asset, and delivery coordination

Strong teams are built through trust, clarity, and empathy.

I take a people-centered approach to PM. I pay attention not just to what needs to be delivered, but to how the team is working, where people feel blocked, and what support they need to do their best work.

  • Building trust and open communication within the team
  • Connecting artists, designers, developers, clients, and stakeholders
  • Recognizing workload concerns and blockers early
  • Facilitating constructive discussions and resolving misalignment
  • Supporting team morale while maintaining accountability
  • Keeping decisions, responsibilities, and progress transparent

Finding practical paths through creative complexity.

My background in design and digital media helps me understand both the creative intention behind a project and the production realities required to deliver it. I help turn uncertainty into clear, actionable next steps.

  • Creative problem-solving within production constraints
  • Translating broad ideas into achievable production steps
  • Supporting design, art, technical, and production discussions
  • Synthesizing feedback into focused priorities
  • Visual communication of concepts, workflows, and progress
  • Balancing creative ambition, quality, scope, and feasibility
